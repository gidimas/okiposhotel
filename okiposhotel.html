Welcome to O Kipos Hotel

Experience the warmth of Greek hospitality at O Kipos Hotel. Our name means "The Garden" in Greek, reflecting the lush, tranquil surroundings that make our property a true oasis for travelers.

Nestled in a picturesque setting, our boutique hotel offers comfortable accommodations, stunning views, and the genuine warmth of Mediterranean hospitality.
